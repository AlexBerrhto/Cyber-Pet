# 🐾 CyberPet: Your Desk-Side Digital Companion

Ever get bored staring at code and wish you had a pet to keep you company? But your office (or landlord) says "No Pets Allowed"? 

**CyberPet** is the solution. It's a lightweight, interactive digital pet that lives right in your browser. Feed it, play with it, and keep it happy while you work.

## 🛠️ The Tech Stack
This project is built to be easily customizable for anyone who knows the basics of Python and JavaScript:
* **Frontend:** React (JavaScript) – The "face" of your pet.
* **Backend:** FastAPI (Python) – The "brain" and logic.
* **Database:** SQLite – Remembers your pet's name and hunger levels.
